Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with FX Graphics

What is your typical project timeline? +

Timelines vary based on project scope. Logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks, complete branding 3-4 weeks, and larger projects 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure? +

We offer both project-based pricing and retainer packages. Most projects range from $500 for simple designs to $5,000+ for comprehensive branding. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.

How many revisions do you include? +

We include 3 rounds of revisions for all design concepts. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed. Our process is collaborative, and we work closely with you to ensure complete satisfaction.

What file formats do you deliver? +

We deliver all standard file formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG, and SVG. For web projects, we provide optimized files. We also include source files and usage guidelines with every project.
