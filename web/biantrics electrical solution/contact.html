Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?

For emergency electrical issues, we aim to have a technician at your location within 60 minutes in most Nairobi areas. We prioritize emergencies to ensure your safety and restore power as quickly as possible.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all electrical work. For simple jobs, we can often give you a quote over the phone. For more complex projects, we'll schedule a free on-site assessment.

Are your electricians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our electricians are fully licensed, insured, and regularly trained on the latest electrical codes and safety standards. We carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service Nairobi and its surrounding areas. This includes Westlands, Karen, Langata, Kileleshwa, Kilimani, Runda, Lavington, South B/C, and many other neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

Yes, we offer a 2-year warranty on all our electrical workmanship. Additionally, all materials and parts we install come with their manufacturer warranties. We stand behind the quality of our work.
