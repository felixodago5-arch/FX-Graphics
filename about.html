Our Story

FX Graphics was founded in 2024 as a focused creative studio with a clear goal to help businesses bring their ideas to life through thoughtful, effective design. We work with a flexible, collaborative approach that allows each project to receive personal attention and creative care.

I’m Felix, the Marketing & Online Design Specialist at FX Graphics. I manage client communication, digital presence, and portfolio presentation, ensuring every project is clearly represented and reaches the right audience.

When needed, FX Graphics collaborates with trusted creatives to support specific project requirements. This approach allows us to combine creativity, strategy, and attention to detail while maintaining consistency and quality across all work. From logos and social media graphics to posters and flyers, every project is designed to look great and deliver real business results.
